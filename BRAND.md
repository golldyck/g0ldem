# G0LDEM — Brand & Narrative

**Tagline:** *Forge golden minds on 0G.*
**Accent:** gold / amber `#C9920F` on a warm-dark theme.

## The name decodes into three meanings

| Part | Source | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Gold** | founder's handle (**Goldy**) | value, gold, the top tier of the system |
| **Golem** | the myth | a man-made autonomous being given a *soul* and **owned** — the metaphor for an AI agent |
| **0G** | golden zero in place of the "O" → G**0**LDEM | the 0G network + *zero gravity* = weightless ownership of value, without friction |

Reads as **"Goldem"** — sounds like ***gold 'em*** → **"own them, collect them."**
The name itself is a call to **own and collect** your agents.

## Why "golem" maps 1:1 onto the product

In the legend, a golem is shaped from clay and brought to life by inserting a written
word — the **shem (שם)**; remove the word and the golem powers off. That maps cleanly:

| Lore | What it is in G0LDEM |
|---|---|
| clay | the base LLM on 0G Compute |
| **shem** (the word that animates) | the persona prompt = the encrypted "brain" |
| bringing the golem to life | **forge** (mint INFT) |
| the maker owns & commands the golem | INFT ownership (ERC-7857) |
| the word can be pulled out and handed over | the brain re-encrypts and moves to the new owner |
| the seal/mark on the golem | **Soul Sigil** — deterministic avatar from `persona_hash` |

## Terminology: lore in the UI, tech in the code

Keep technical terms in code (`mint`, `encryptedURI`); use the lore in UI & narrative.

| Lore (UI/narrative) | Technical (code) |
|---|---|
| Golem | agent / INFT |
| Shem (soul) | encrypted brain (persona + skills + memory) |
| Seal / Sigil | deterministic avatar from `persona_hash` |
| Forge | mint |
| Golem market | marketplace |

## The pitch in one breath

> You shape a golem, breathe in its soul (the *shem*), and forge it to life — and you own
> the **mind itself**: verifiable (0G Compute), portable (it moves when you sell it), private
> (encrypted in 0G Storage). Not a speculative token over a black box — **True AI Ownership
> vs API Access.** Forge golden minds on 0G.
